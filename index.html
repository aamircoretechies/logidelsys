Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI dispatch engine handle traffic delays? expand_more

LogiAI integrates real-time traffic data feeds and historical transit patterns to dynamically adjust routes. If a delay is detected, the system automatically recalibrates ETAs and notifies stakeholders.

Can we integrate LogiAI with our current ERP/CRM? expand_more

Yes. We provide a robust REST API and pre-built connectors for major platforms like SAP, Oracle, and Salesforce, ensuring a seamless data flow between systems.

Is our operational data secure? expand_more

Security is our priority. LogiAI is SOC2 Type II compliant and utilizes AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit.
